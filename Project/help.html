How to play this game

Press the settings button to choose the difficulty.
(按設置選擇難度)

You may choose the character in settings.
(您可以選擇設置中的角色。)

Press cheat code button (The red one) to enter cheat codes to activate secert features.
(按作弊輸入作弊碼以激活額外的功能。)

Click the glass to choose the way you go.
